A Glimpse into Our History

Twiga Resort was founded in 2018 with a vision to create a sanctuary that perfectly blends nature’s tranquility with modern luxury.
Starting as a small family-owned establishment by the lakeside, the resort quickly became known for its warm hospitality, breathtaking views, and exceptional culinary offerings.
Our dedication to providing exceptional experiences has allowed us to grow into a well-loved getaway for visitors from all over the world.

Our Mission and Values

At Twiga Resort, we are committed to providing a unique and personalized experience to every guest. Our mission is to create a place where luxury meets nature, offering comfort, relaxation, and adventure. We value sustainability and are proud of our eco-friendly practices that help us reduce our environmental impact. Every service we provide is designed to leave our guests with unforgettable memories.

We believe in the power of community and actively work to support local businesses and artisans. From sourcing fresh ingredients from nearby farms to offering locally crafted decor, our resort reflects the culture and spirit of the region. Our team is dedicated to offering unparalleled service, ensuring that each guest feels like part of the Twiga family.
We are constantly evolving and finding new ways to enrich your stay, ensuring that every visit feels special and exceeds your expectations. Our passion is to create unforgettable experiences that make you feel right at home.
